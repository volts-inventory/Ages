## Q30 — Split reach and persistence into two ladders
**Status:** Decided
**Context:** Initial proposal merged "writing" with "communication
reach." But persistence (durability across time) and reach (extension
across space) advance somewhat independently and tend to use different
phenomena.
**Decision:** Two ladders. Persistence T0–T4 governs how knowledge
survives generations. Reach R0–R4 governs how live signalling extends
beyond biological default. Both have *forms* derived per civ; both
multiply into transmission-loss formulas (persistence into time-loss,
reach into space-loss). Reach matters most in M5 with multi-civ
contact.
**Lives in:** `docs/civ.md` Discovery → Persistence ladder +
Communication-reach ladder.
