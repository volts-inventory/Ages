## Q6 — Discovery formula
**Status:** Decided (extended by Q32, retargeted by Q40)
**Context:** Initial proposal used `score = base × intelligence ×
min(depth_a, depth_b) × supporting-density × curiosity-bonus`
accumulating per tick. User flagged this as a dice roll in disguise.
**Decision:** Replaced with a six-phase observation-driven pipeline:
cohort baseline observations → per-figure observations → pattern
detection → hypothesis testing → discovery → adoption + decay. Cultural-
influence hooks wired in at three points (investigation gating, focus
redirection, hypothesis suppression), stubbed as no-ops in M3 and
filled in M4. Phases C–E upgraded to quantitative fitting against
functional forms (Q32). With the Q40 pivot, the data being fit comes
from observations of real physics state surfaced through the recognition
layer rather than authored true_formulas — the math is the same, the
ground truth is the actual simulated physics.
**Lives in:** `docs/civ.md` Discovery section.
