## Q18 — Species derivation order
**Status:** Decided
**Context:** Should species be sampled independently of the planet, or
derived from it?
**Decision:** Derived. Species evolves from {planet, regions,
physically-present recognized phenomena} — its body plan, sensorium,
and lifespan reflect the ecological niche. Compatibility is a hard
constraint by construction. Species is therefore derived *after* the
physics has spun up to equilibrium and the recognition-template library
has been filtered to physical-presence templates for the run.
**Lives in:** `docs/world.md` Species + Run start order.
