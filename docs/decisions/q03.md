## Q3 — Named figures cap
**Status:** Decided (refined by Q47)
**Context:** Named figures are the only individuals modeled; cap
controls discovery throughput.
**Decision:** Scaling cap. Starts at 2–3 for a founding band; grows
with population × literacy × institution count. Hard ceiling of 200
active discoverers **per active civ** (not per species — multiple
concurrent civs can each have their own active discoverers, per Q47).
Already-contributed figures persist in the history log indefinitely,
even after their civ collapses.
**Lives in:** `docs/civ.md` Individuals section.
