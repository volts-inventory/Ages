## Q23 — Cultural influence on discovery (hooks)
**Status:** Decided
**Context:** Culture should be able to suppress or redirect discovery,
not just filter transmission downstream.
**Decision:** Three hooks placed in the discovery pipeline:
- Investigation gating (phase A and B): cultural taboo can suppress
  observation.
- Focus redirection (phase B): civ-level cultural priority vector
  biases named-figure focus.
- Hypothesis suppression (phase D): cosmology-mismatched hypotheses
  accumulate confidence more slowly.
M3 stubs each as no-ops (the milestone where the pipeline first
lands). Substantive numbers tracked separately as Q9 and Q24–Q26 and
fill in during M4.
**Lives in:** `docs/civ.md` Discovery section.
