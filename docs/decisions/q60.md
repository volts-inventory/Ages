## Q60 — Catastrophe events
**Status:** Decided (M4-minimal: deterministic per-tick volcanic
+ disease triggers on planet/civ state; Q48-v2 reads them as
post-catastrophe founding triggers; v2 adds climatic + impact
events)
**Context:** PLANNING M4 + Q48 reference "post-catastrophe
reset" as a founding trigger; without a catastrophe-event
mechanism the trigger can't fire. Q60 ships the minimal
catastrophe taxonomy that downstream Qs can consume.

**Decision:** Two catastrophe kinds in M4-min:

1. **Volcanic eruption.** Fires when *any* hex cell's charge
   magnitude crosses a high threshold while temperature is
   extreme — proxy for a tectonically-active region. Effect:
   drop the cell's `Substance::Fuel` density to zero (biomass
   destroyed) and lower its temperature by 50 K (post-eruption
   cooling); 5% of civ population lost.
2. **Disease outbreak.** Fires when civ population exceeds 80%
   of `carrying_capacity` AND the civ has been continuously
   active for ≥300 ticks (crowding-driven disease). Effect:
   30% of civ population lost (capped at floor 10); cosmology
   push toward `(communitarian + mystical)` to model historical
   plague-cosmology pivots.

**Triggers and bookkeeping:**
- Both check fires at most once per civ per `cooldown` ticks
  (per-civ `last_volcanic_tick`, `last_disease_tick`).
  Cooldown 200 ticks for volcanic, 500 for disease — placeholders
  under Q28.
- `CatastropheFired` event carries `(tick, civ_id, kind, magnitude_q32)`
  where magnitude is the population-fraction lost.
- `last_collapse_tick` (used by Q48 founding) is *not* updated
  by catastrophes — they're not collapses, they're stresses.
  Q48-v2's post-catastrophe trigger reads
  `last_catastrophe_tick` instead.

**Why these two specifically:**
- Volcanic uses the existing physics-state proxies (temperature,
  charge); no new physics required.
- Disease uses Q22 carrying capacity + civ age — both already
  wired.
- Together they deliver the project goal "different worlds,
  different sciences" — a sub-surface-ocean planet with no
  lightning has very different volcanic frequencies than an
  Earth-like one.

**Deferred to v2:**
- Climatic catastrophe (long-term temperature shift): needs
  Q57 settlement-pop for regional temperature tracking.
- Impact catastrophe (asteroid strike): needs orbital model
  beyond M4 scope.
- Per-cell propagation: catastrophe spreads to adjacent cells
  over time. Needs Q57.

**Lives in:** `sim/civ/catastrophe.rs` (kinds + trigger checks
+ effects), `protocol::CatastropheFired`, `sim/core::CivLifecycle`
phase consumer (right after Q49 collapse check).
