## Q4 — Lifespan model
**Status:** Decided
**Context:** Initial draft used flat 50–70 ticks; biology of the species
should drive baseline.
**Decision:** Expanded to a full Species object derived from the planet.
Lifespan = species baseline × civ medicine multiplier × per-figure noise.
Species traits (body plan, sensorium, respiration, lifespan baseline,
maturity, sociality, cognition baseline) all flow from planet + region +
physically-present recognized phenomena.
**Lives in:** `docs/world.md` Species section, `docs/civ.md` Individuals
section.
