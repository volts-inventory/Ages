## Q7 — Transmission-loss rates
**Status:** Decided
**Context:** Civ knowledge decays as it transmits across generations.
Initial draft used flat `30% oral / 5% written` numbers. "Oral" is
mouth-anchored and wrong for limb-percussion, bioluminescent, chemical,
or any non-vocal modality.
**Decision:** Replaced flat numbers with a derived formula:
`loss_per_generation = species.t0_loss_per_generation ×
persistence_tech_modifier × cultural_modifier`. The species baseline is
itself derived at run start from cognition, sociality, lifespan, and
communication channel. Persistence tech is generalised to a tier ladder
T0–T4 whose *form* (writing, ice-cuneiform, knotted cords, planted
patterns, songlines, bioluminescent grids…) is computed per civ from
species manipulation modes + regional materials.
**Lives in:** `docs/civ.md` Discovery → F. Adoption and decay,
`docs/world.md` Species → Communication channel + Manipulation modes.
