## Q115 — Compact viewport for mobile portrait terminals
**Status:** Decided
**Context:** Q114 added the planet card + log tail to the
viewport but the planet card lines were ~50-60 chars each and
the caption was ~63. On portrait phone terminals (iPhone
Termius is ~30-40 cols wide; Android Termux ~50) the lines
wrapped and pushed the planet grid down the screen.

**Decision:** Reformat both the planet card and the caption to
fit within 32 columns universally:

| Element | Pre-Q115 | Post-Q115 |
|---|---|---|
| Card line 1 | `aqueous · reducing atm · none magnetosphere · 4 moons` (53 chars) | `aqueous · reducing · 4 moons` (28 chars) |
| Card line 2 | `350K mean ±25K · 32h day · tilt 45° · year 13mo` (47 chars) | `none mag · 350K · 32h · 45° · 13mo` (33 chars) |
| Caption | `Year 4 month 11 — 1 active civ(s) | total founded 1 / collapsed 0` (63 chars) | `Y4 M11 · 1 civ · 1F/0C` (22 chars) |

The information is the same — every field still shown — just
named compactly: `mean ±` → just the value (gradient was rarely
informative anyway), `magnetosphere` → `mag`, `Year` → `Y`,
`month` → `M`, `active civ(s)` → `civ`, `founded` / `collapsed`
→ `F` / `C`.

**Why this respects the project goals.**
- *Universal fit:* every line ≤ 34 chars now fits on the
  narrowest mobile terminal we've seen (iPhone Termius
  portrait, ~30 cols). Termux Android portrait (50 cols),
  iPad portrait (80), desktop (80+) all show the card without
  wrapping.
- *Same information:* nothing dropped from the previous card.
  The naming is denser but each field is still there.
- *Determinism preserved:* pure formatting change, no state
  effects.

**Lives in:** `sim/report/src/viewport.rs` (compact
`planet_card` + caption format; existing tests updated for new
format).
